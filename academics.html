Teaching Approach

At St Anne's Prep, we employ a student-centered teaching approach that recognizes and nurtures individual learning styles. Our methodology includes:

  • Interactive Learning: Engaging lessons that encourage active participation, questions, and discussion
  • Hands-On Activities: Practical experiments, projects, and real-world applications of concepts
  • Technology Integration: Use of educational technology to enhance learning experiences
  • Differentiated Instruction: Tailored teaching methods to address diverse learning needs
  • Collaborative Learning: Group work and peer learning to develop teamwork and communication skills
  • Continuous Assessment: Regular feedback and support to track and improve student progress

Assessment Methods

We employ a comprehensive assessment system that goes beyond traditional examinations to evaluate student learning holistically:

Formative Assessment

Ongoing evaluation through class participation, homework, projects, quizzes, and practical work. This helps teachers identify learning gaps early and adjust instruction accordingly.

Summative Assessment

End-of-term examinations that measure mastery of curriculum objectives and provide feedback on overall academic progress.

Performance-Based Assessment

Evaluation of practical skills through presentations, demonstrations, creative work, and project-based learning activities.

Progress Reports

Detailed reports issued three times per year, documenting academic performance, effort, behavior, and teacher recommendations. Parent-teacher conferences provide opportunities for discussion and collaboration.

Academic Support

We are committed to ensuring every student reaches their full potential. Our academic support includes:

  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
  • Extra coaching for students who need additional support
  • Enrichment programs for advanced learners
  • Regular parent communication and progress updates
  • Study skills and time management guidance
  • Access to library and learning resources

Academic Calendar

The academic year at St Anne's Prep runs from January to November, divided into three terms:

  • Term 1: January to April
  • Term 2: May to August
  • Term 3: September to November

Each term includes mid-term assessments and ends with comprehensive examinations. School holidays align with the national calendar, allowing families to plan accordingly.
