Student Life

Where learning extends beyond the classroom

A Vibrant School Community

At St Anne's Prep, education goes beyond textbooks and examinations. We believe in developing well-rounded individuals through a rich variety of extracurricular activities, clubs, sports, and programs that nurture talents, build character, and create lasting memories.

Clubs & Societies

Our diverse range of clubs provides students with opportunities to explore interests, develop new skills, and connect with peers who share their passions.

Science Club

Hands-on experiments, science fairs, and exploration of scientific concepts beyond the regular curriculum.

Debate Society

Developing public speaking, critical thinking, and argumentation skills through structured debates and competitions.

Environmental Club

Tree planting, recycling initiatives, and environmental awareness programs that teach students about sustainability.

Drama Club

Theater productions, acting workshops, and creative expression through performance arts.

Music & Choir

Vocal training, instrumental lessons, and performance opportunities at school events and competitions.

Journalism Club

School newsletter production, writing workshops, and media literacy development.

Sports & Athletics

Physical education is an integral part of our curriculum. We offer a comprehensive sports program that promotes fitness, teamwork, discipline, and healthy competition.

Sports Offered

  • Football: Inter-house competitions and regional tournaments
  • Netball: Team training and competitive matches
  • Volleyball: Skills development and school leagues
  • Basketball: Court practice and friendly matches
  • Athletics: Track and field events, cross-country running
  • Table Tennis: Indoor games and tournaments
  • Swimming: Basic swimming and water safety (seasonal)

Our sports facilities include well-maintained playing fields, courts, and equipment. Students participate in inter-school competitions, developing both athletic skills and sportsmanship.

Cultural & Creative Programs

We celebrate creativity and cultural expression through various programs that allow students to showcase their artistic talents.

Music Programs

Our music department offers instruction in both vocal and instrumental music. Students participate in choir performances, music festivals, and school concerts.

Visual Arts

Students explore drawing, painting, sculpture, and crafts in our well-equipped art room. Regular exhibitions showcase student work.

Cultural Days

We celebrate Kenya's rich cultural diversity through special events featuring traditional dance, music, food, and dress from various communities.

Student Leadership

Leadership development is central to our student life program. We provide opportunities for students to take responsibility and develop leadership skills.

Student Council

Democratically elected student representatives who voice student concerns, organize events, and serve as liaisons between students and administration.

Prefects System

Senior students serve as prefects, helping maintain discipline, mentoring younger students, and assisting in school operations.

Class Representatives

Each class elects representatives who coordinate class activities and communicate with teachers.

Student Support Services

We are committed to supporting the overall well-being of our students through comprehensive support services.

Guidance & Counseling

Trained counselors provide support for academic challenges, personal issues, and career guidance. Confidential counseling sessions are available to all students.

Peer Support Program

Older students are trained to support younger peers, creating a culture of kindness and mutual assistance.

Health Services

Basic first aid and health monitoring are available on campus. We maintain close communication with parents regarding student health matters.

Learning Support

Additional assistance for students who need extra help with specific subjects or learning challenges.

School Events

Throughout the academic year, we organize events that bring our community together and create memorable experiences:

  • Sports Day - Annual athletics competition
  • Academic Day - Subject exhibitions and presentations
  • Cultural Festival - Celebration of diversity
  • Music & Drama Concerts - Student performances
  • Science Fair - Student research projects
  • Career Day - Exposure to various professions
  • Awards Day - Recognition of student achievements
  • Parent-Teacher Days - Community engagement
