About St Anne's Prep

Building a legacy of excellence in education since 2009

Our Story

St Anne's Prep was established in 2009 with a clear vision: to provide quality, accessible education that prepares students for success in an ever-changing world. What began as a small institution with a handful of dedicated teachers and eager students has grown into a thriving educational community serving over 500 learners.

Throughout our journey, we have remained committed to our founding principles of academic excellence, character development, and holistic education. Our growth has been guided by the belief that every child deserves an environment where they can discover their potential, develop their talents, and build the confidence to pursue their dreams.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution recognized for nurturing well-rounded, innovative, and responsible global citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that combines academic rigor with character formation, empowering students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for lifelong success and meaningful contribution to their communities.

Core Values

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in everything we do, from teaching and learning to student support and facility management.

Integrity

We cultivate honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all interactions, modeling the values we wish to instill in our students.

Respect

We honor the dignity, diversity, and potential of every individual within our community, fostering an inclusive environment.

Innovation

We embrace creative approaches to education, continuously improving our methods to meet the needs of modern learners.

Community

We build strong partnerships between students, parents, teachers, and the wider community to support student success.

Responsibility

We encourage accountability, leadership, and service, preparing students to make positive contributions to society.

Leadership & Administration

Our school is led by a team of experienced educators and administrators who bring decades of combined expertise in curriculum development, student welfare, and educational leadership. The leadership team works collaboratively to ensure that St Anne's Prep maintains its high standards while continuously evolving to meet the needs of our students.

Our Board of Governors provides strategic oversight, ensuring fiscal responsibility, policy compliance, and alignment with our mission and values. Together with our administrative team, they create an environment where excellence thrives.

Accreditation & Registration

St Anne's Prep is fully registered with the Ministry of Education and adheres to all national curriculum standards and regulatory requirements. We undergo regular quality assurance reviews and maintain compliance with educational best practices.

Our commitment to quality has earned us recognition within the educational community, and we continue to invest in teacher development, infrastructure, and student resources to uphold these standards.

Why Choose St Anne's Prep

  • Experienced, qualified, and passionate teaching staff
  • Safe, supportive, and inclusive learning environment
  • Balanced curriculum combining academics, arts, and sports
  • Modern facilities including laboratories, library, and sports grounds
  • Strong emphasis on character development and life skills
  • Active parent-teacher collaboration
  • Track record of academic success and student progression
  • Commitment to continuous improvement and innovation
